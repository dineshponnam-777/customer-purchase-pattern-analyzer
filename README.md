# 📊 Customer Purchase Pattern Analyzer

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The Customer Purchase Pattern Analyzer is a data analytics and business intelligence project developed using SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, regional sales performance, payment method usage, and customer segmentation insights.

The dashboard provides interactive visual analytics that help identify high-performing product categories, customer spending patterns, and overall business performance.

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 🛍️ Product Category Analysis

- Grocery category generated the highest revenue.
- Fashion category contributed the lowest revenue share.

### 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis

- West region recorded the highest sales revenue.
- East region showed the lowest overall contribution.

### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis

- Budget customers generated the maximum revenue.
- Premium customers contributed strong mid-level sales performance.

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis

- UPI and Credit Card were the most preferred payment methods.
- Debit Card usage was comparatively lower.

### 🔄 Purchase Frequency Analysis

- Quarterly customers represented the largest customer group.
- Weekly purchase frequency showed lower customer participation.

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Age Group Analysis

- Customers aged 46+ contributed the highest revenue.
- Younger age groups generated comparatively lower revenue.
